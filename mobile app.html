Native Application

Native apps, such as iOS or Android, are called so because they are developed for particular platforms. If you're thinking about going worldwide, for each platform, you'll have to create separate versions of your app, made by different coders. Native apps' safety and security are guaranteed by the approval of an app store on which they have been released.
